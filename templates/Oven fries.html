    • Oven fries with a salad (Main course)
    Preparation

    1 Person

    Ingredients (Present in mealbox):

    50 gr - Cauliflower rice
    65 gr - Carrots
    250 gr - Potatoes
    0.5 Teaspoon(s) of Bellpepper powder
    0.25 Cucumber
    62.5 gr - Beetroots
    3.8 gr - Fresh dill
    12.5 gr - Raisins

    Ingredients (Not present in mealbox):
    25 ml Yoghurt
    Salt & Olive oil
    Mayonaise & Lemon & Baking paper
    Allergies: Lactose, Spices, (Raisins)

    8 g Protein
    9 g Fibers
    18 g Sugar
    35 g Fat
    41 Kcal Carbohydrates
    400 Mg Sodium
    600 Kcal Nutrition

    Preparation time: 30 min
    (based on 2 persons)
    Ease of making: Easy
    Liked by 65% of our enthusiastic cooks
    * Preheat the oven to 200°C.
    * Peel the potatoes and cut them into slices of about 0.5 cm. Then cut them into strips of about 1 cm.
    * Rinse the fries under cold running water. Place them on a dry tea towel, place another tea towel on top and dry the fries well.
    * Spread the fries on a baking tray lined with baking paper, sprinkle with 3/5 of the oil, the paprika and 1/2 Teaspoon of salt.
    * Toss and spread in a single layer on the baking sheet. Bake the chips in the middle of the oven for about 35 minutes until they are golden and crispy. Turn after 20 minutes.
    * Meanwhile, mix the mayonnaise with the yogurt into a creamy sauce and cut the cucumber in half lengthwise and scrape out the seeds with a teaspoon.
    * Grate the carrots and cucumber on a coarse grater and mix in a bowl with the grated beetroot and cauliflower rice.
    * Scrub the lime clean, grate the green skin with a fine grater, halve it and squeeze. After that finely chop the dill.
    * Mix the rest of the oil with 1/2 tsp lime zest and 1/2 tbsp lime juice (for 2 people) and mix with the dill and raisins through the grated vegetables. Season with pepper and possibly salt.
    * Serve the fries with the raw vegetables and yogurt mayonnaise.
    The meal is now ready !
