    • Pancakes (Main course/Side course/Dessert)
    Preparation

    1 Person

    Ingredients (Present in mealbox):

    1 - Egg(s)
    15 gr - Unsalted butter
    150 gr - Flour
    250 ml Halffull milk

    Ingredients (Not present in mealbox):
    Salt, (cinnamon)
    Allergies: Lactose, eggs and gluten

    1 G Salt
    2 G Fibers
    5 G Fat
    9 G Protein
    30 Kcal Carbohydrates
    160 Mg Sodium
    205 Kcal Nutrition

    Preparation time: 15 min
    (based on 4 persons)
    Ease of making: Easy
    Liked by 72% of our enthusiastic cooks
    * Sift the flour with the salt into a large mixing bowl.
    * Add the eggs and half of the milk, beat with a whisk until smooth.
    * Pour in the rest of the milk and beat again until smooth.
    * Cover the bowl with foil and let the batter stand for 20 minutes. Continue after you have waited the 20 minutes.
    * Heat a knob of butter in a 20 cm frying pan and spoon a ladle of batter into it. Rotate the pan so that the entire bottom is covered.
    * Bake the pancake for 2 minutes on medium heat until the top is dry and the bottom is golden brown. After flip the pancake and cook for 1 more minute.
    * Keep the pancakes warm under a lid or a plate.
    * Repeat the previous two steps until there is no more batter left.
    The meal is now ready !
    For an extra touch on the vegan kapsalon try adding some Cinnamon to the batter.
