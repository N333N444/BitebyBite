    • Vegan Kapsalon (Main course)
    Preparation

    1 Person

    Ingredients (Present in mealbox):

    50 gr - Carrots
    75 gr - Vegetable cheese
    87.5 gr - Vegan shoarma
    97.5 gr - Radish
    100 gr - Oven fries
    0.5 - Cucumber
    0.25 Teaspoon(s) of Fresh parsley

    Ingredients (Not present in mealbox):
    Tablespoons white wine vinegar
    Salt
    Vegan garlic sauce
    Allergies: -

    31 Kcal Carbohydrates
    500 Kcal Calories
    3.4 G Fibers
    2.2 G Salt

    Preparation time: 25 min
    (based on 2 persons)
    Ease of making: Easy
    Liked by 87% of our enthusiastic cooks
    * Warm the oven at the temperature noted at the bag of oven fries.
    * Cut the radish in small parts.
    If needed cut the big radishes in halfs first.
    * Grater the carrots and cut the cucumbers in thin half moons.
    * Add the rest of the salad ingredients (carrots, cucumbers, radish, vinegar) and toss them around in a bowl. Then store it in a sealed container in the refrigerator.
    * Divide the oven fries over the baking tray so that you have one layer. Bake according to the method of preparation. Toss once halfway through.
    * Take a large frying pan and fry the vegan shawarma on medium/high heat for about 5 to 10 minutes until they are golden brown.
    * Remove the fries from the oven and spread a layer of fries in an oven dish. Divide some cheese over it and then fries again. Repeat this until you have no more fries left.
    * Then divide the shawarma over it and some more cheese. Place the dish in the oven for about 5 minutes until the cheese has melted. While this is going on roughly chop the parsley.
    * As soon as the cheese has melted, divide the salad, parsley, garlic sauce and possibly sambal over it.
    The meal is now ready !
    For an extra touch on the vegan kapsalon try adding some selfmade vegan garlic sauce.
