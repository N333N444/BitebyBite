    * Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and oil an baking dish for the casserole.
    * Boil the potato slices in a large pan of water for 5 minutes and then drain the water.
    * Grater the carrots and cut the cucumbers in thin half moons.
    * Fry the leek briefly in a pan with one tablespoon of olive oil for 5 minutes until it starts to soften slightly.
    * In a bowl, whisk together the crème frache, eggs, mustard, pepper, salt and parsley.
    * Cut the salmon in half horizontally once or twice to get thin slices and spread half of the potato slices over the bottom of the oiled baking dish.
    * Sprinkle with a little bit of salt and pepper. Place the salmon and spoon the fried leek over this.
    * Pour half of the egg mixture over this (Made three steps ago).
    * Cover with potato slices and pour over the rest of the egg mixture and sprinkle with some cheese. Put the casserole in the oven for around 30 min until the cheese has melted and the potato slices are well done.
    The meal is now ready !
